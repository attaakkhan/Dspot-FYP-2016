About DSpot


Dspot Social IOT is a social network application.The Internet of Things (IOT)is an environment in which objects are provided with unique identifiers and the ability to transfer data over a network without requiring human-to-human or human-to-computer interaction.

Each device assigned a unique IP. It is used to communicate not only people but also common appliances using social networking website. Application would be free for all and would be free to download from any device having the application.

Dspot has some features similar to facebook like maintaining profile, chatting with friends, sharing pictures. Apart from this, dspot includes devices to userâ€™s Account. Users will be able to control their appliances (synchronization) & share the accessibility with certain people of their will. User will be able to save his time by eliminating the practice of on/off of common appliances manually/automatically or using sensors. Moreover, the application relies on the internet. Centralization of workstations will be done from this application.
